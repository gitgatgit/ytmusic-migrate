📜 Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2026

1. Introduction

YTMusic Migrate ("we", "our", "the tool") is a personal, open-source CLI tool for migrating YouTube Music data between user accounts. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

We do NOT store any of your data. This tool runs entirely on your local machine.

When you use YTMusic Migrate with your Google account:

  • You authenticate directly with Google OAuth
  • OAuth tokens are stored locally on YOUR computer at ~/.ytmusic_migrate/tokens.json
  • We never transmit, store, or access your data on our servers
  • YouTube/Google controls what data is accessible via their API

3. How We Use Your Information

YTMusic Migrate only uses the YouTube Data API v3 to:

  • Read your playlists, liked videos, watch later, and subscriptions (with your explicit OAuth consent)
  • Create new playlists on your target account
  • Add videos to playlists on your target account
  • Subscribe to channels on your target account

All operations are performed on your behalf with your credentials.

4. Data Storage & Security

No cloud storage. All data remains on your local device.

OAuth tokens are cached locally for convenience but can be deleted at any time by removing ~/.ytmusic_migrate/tokens.json.

5. Google Privacy Policy

This tool uses Google's OAuth 2.0 system. Google's privacy practices are governed by their Privacy Policy.

6. Data Deletion

To delete all local data:

rm -rf ~/.ytmusic_migrate

This removes all cached OAuth tokens and migration state from your computer.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

8. Contact Us
